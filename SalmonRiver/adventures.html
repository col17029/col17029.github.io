Salmon River Rapids Tour: SRRT

Are you ready to brave the rapids, exert yourself to exhaustion, and have the time of your life? Come prepared to work hard and play hard on the Salmon River rapids.

Length of tour: 6 hours

Skill Level: Experienced

Cost: $300/person

What we provide:

Our certified river guides can take you and your group on one of Idaho's most popular rapids adventure. We supply the raft, safety gear, and mentor. You supply the determination.

Salmon River Kayaking: SRK

Not quite ready for the rapids? We offer 2 hour tours in which you learn the basics of a kayak, explore the water and surrounding streams, and enjoy the freedom a kayak can offer. Like to fish? Grab a pole and troll the river as you go.

Length of tour: 2 hours

Skill Level: Beginner

Cost: $100/person

What we provide:

Our trained tour guides can give you expert advice on how to use a kayak, from entering and exiting, to survival techniques, they can walk you through what works and doesn't work to give you the best experience on the river. Consider this tour the bunny hill of kayaking.

Salmon River Kayak3: SRK3

Combine kayaking with camping and you get an amazing 3-day tour. Enjoy kayaking 20 miles each day exploring the river and local wildlife. Setup camp and watch the stars while you rest up for the next days leg of the river.

Then you've come to the right place.

You'll have one of the state's best tour guides to help you along the way.

Length of tour: 3 Day/2 Night

Skill Level: Intermediate

Cost: $500/person

What we provide:

Bring your camping necessities, snacks and hydration packs, we'll provide the rest. Pack light and keep it water tight, you'll be lugging it with you along the water way. One of our trained tour guides will be with you the whole time, giving kayak pointers on maneuvering and helping get gear down the river. If you ask nice, you might be able to talk them into a campfire song or story. The pickup point can be reached easily on the 3rd day in which we will drive you back to the lodge.
